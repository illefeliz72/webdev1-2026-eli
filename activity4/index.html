"It is Where Art Starts"

The Huion Kamvas 13 Gen 3 transforms your creative process by delivering smooth, lag-free drawing with a natural paper-like feel. Its compact design and advanced PenTech 4.0 make professional digital art accessible anywhere, empowering artists to bring their visions to life effortlessly.

Features

  • Upgraded PenTech 4.0:

    The included stylus utilizes Huion's latest pen technology with an astonishing 16,384 levels of pressure sensitivity, tilt support, and a highly responsive initial activation force of just 2g for incredibly precise line consistency.

  • Full-Laminated Display:

    The 13.3-inch IPS panel eliminates parallax for a more natural pen-to-screen feel, accurately covering 99% of the sRGB color gamut. Its Canvas Glass finish prevents glare while providing a satisfying, paper-like texture.

  • Dual Dial Controllers:

    To drastically speed up your workflow, the tablet includes five customizable silent press keys and two built-in dial controllers. You can easily program them for adjusting brush sizes, zooming in on the canvas, or scrubbing through timelines.
